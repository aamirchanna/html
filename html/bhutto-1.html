Trribute to 44th death anniversary of Zulfikar Ali Bhutto to be observed on April 4

" Commissioner Larkana Division Ghanwer Ali Leghari on Mon­day chaired a high-level meet­ing at the Conference room of his office here. The meeting re­viewed and discussed in detail regarding the arrangements to observe the 44th death anni­versary of former prime min­ister and founder Chairman of Pakistan Peoples Party, Sha­heed Zulfikar Ali Bhutto which to be held at Garhi Khuda Bux Bhutto on April 4. The meeting was attended among others by Deputy Commissioner Larkana, Rabiaya Siyal, Deputy Commis­sioner Kamber-Shahdadkot, SSP Larkana, and officers of various departments.
